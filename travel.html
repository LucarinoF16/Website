Travel

Arrival by plane/train/public transportation

From Munich Central Station, take the S7 in the direction of Kreuzstraße to Neuperlach Süd or the U5 in the direction of Neuperlach Süd to the Neuperlach Süd terminus.

From the airport, take the S8 to the Ostbahnhof stop and then the S7 or U5 to the Neuperlach Süd stop

From the Neuperlach Süd underground station, take bus 217 or 199 in the direction of Unterhaching or Campeon as far as the 3rd stop Universitätsstraße (approx. 6 min)
