Attendance information

Due to the seminar being hosted by the Institute for Theoretical Computer Science, Mathematics and Operations Research of the University of the Bundeswehr Munich, it will take place on the university campus. The campus of the University of the Bundeswehr Munich is a military exclusion zone. Therefore, citizens from certain countries need a special permit to enter the campus and need to be supervised at all times. The countries in question include: Afghanistan, Algeria, Armenia, Azerbaijan, Belarus, China, Cuba, Georgia, Iran, Iraq, Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Laos, Lebanon, Libya, Moldova, North Korea, Pakistan, Russia, Sudan, Syria, Tajikistan, Turkmenistan, Ukraine, Uzbekistan and Vietnam
